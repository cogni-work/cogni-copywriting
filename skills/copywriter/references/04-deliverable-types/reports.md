# Professional Reports

## Quick Reference
**Purpose:** Comprehensive analysis and documentation
**Length:** Variable (2-100+ pages)
**Formality:** High
**Audience:** Stakeholders, executives, teams
**Best frameworks:** Pyramid, SCQA, Inverted Pyramid
**Key requirement:** Executive summary with answer-first approach

## Standard Structure

### With Pyramid (Research Report)
```markdown
# [Report Title]

## Executive Summary
[Answer-first: Main findings and recommendations]

## Introduction
[Context and scope]

## Methodology
[How analysis was conducted]

## Findings
### Finding 1: [Topic]
[Evidence and analysis]

### Finding 2: [Topic]
[Evidence and analysis]

## Conclusions
[Summary and implications]

## Recommendations
[Actionable next steps]

## Appendices
[Supporting data and details]
```

## Key Characteristics

- **Executive summary mandatory:** Pyramid structure, 1-2 pages
- **Comprehensive:** Full analysis with evidence
- **Well-structured:** Clear hierarchy (max 3 heading levels)
- **Professional formatting:** Tables, charts, citations

## Visual Elements

**Visual density target:** ~1 visual element per 2 paragraphs in body sections (tables, bullets, callouts)

**See also:** [visual-elements.md](../03-formatting-standards/visual-elements.md) - General visual formatting

## Quality Standards

- Flesch 50-60 in executive summary
- Max 3 header levels throughout
- Visual elements: ~1 per 2 paragraphs in body sections
- Executive summary standalone (can read without body)
