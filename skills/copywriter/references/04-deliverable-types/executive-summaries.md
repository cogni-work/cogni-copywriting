# Executive Summaries

## Quick Reference
**Purpose:** Concise summary of longer document
**Length:** 1-2 pages maximum
**Formality:** High
**Audience:** Executives, C-level
**Best frameworks:** BLUF, Pyramid
**Key requirement:** Standalone, answer-first, decision-ready

## Standard Structure

### With Pyramid (Research Summary)
```markdown
# [Document Title]: Executive Summary

## Answer
[Main conclusion in 2-3 sentences]

## Three Key Findings
1. [Finding 1 with metric]
2. [Finding 2 with metric]
3. [Finding 3 with metric]

## Recommendation
[Clear actionable recommendation]

## Next Steps
[Timeline and actions]
```

## Key Characteristics

- **Standalone:** Can read without full document
- **Answer-first:** Pyramid structure mandatory
- **Decision-ready:** Clear recommendations
- **Concise:** 1-2 pages maximum

## Visual Elements

**Visual density target:** 1-2 visual elements maximum (tables, bullets for conciseness)

**See also:** [visual-elements.md](../03-formatting-standards/visual-elements.md) - General visual formatting

## Quality Standards

- 1-2 pages maximum (never exceed)
- Flesch 50-60
- BLUF or Pyramid structure
- Could executive act on this alone?
