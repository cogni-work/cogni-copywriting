# One-Pagers

## Quick Reference
**Purpose:** Single-page product/service summary
**Length:** Exactly 1 page (400-600 words)
**Formality:** Medium
**Audience:** Prospects, executives, stakeholders
**Best frameworks:** PSB, FAB
**Key requirement:** Maximum information density, highly scannable

## Standard Structure

### With PSB
```markdown
# [Compelling Headline]

## The Challenge
[Customer pain point, 2-3 sentences]

## Our Solution
[How you solve it, key features as bullets]

## Your Benefits
- Benefit 1: [quantified impact]
- Benefit 2: [quantified impact]
- Benefit 3: [quantified impact]

## Proof: [Customer testimonial or case study snippet]

## Next Steps: [Clear CTA]
```

## Key Characteristics

- **Exactly 1 page:** Strict constraint
- **High visual density:** Tables, bullets, callouts
- **Scannable:** Can grasp in 30 seconds
- **Focused:** Single topic or offering

## Visual Elements

**Visual density target:** 4-6 visual elements (dense but scannable - bullets, tables, bold emphasis)

**Critical constraint:** Space is premium. Use bullets, tables, and testimonials to maximize impact.

**See also:** [visual-elements.md](../03-formatting-standards/visual-elements.md) - General visual formatting

## Quality Standards

- 400-600 words
- 4-6 visual elements (tables, bullets, callouts)
- Conciseness critical (every word counts)
- Benefits quantified
