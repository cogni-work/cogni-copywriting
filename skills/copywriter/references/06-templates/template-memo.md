## Writing Tips

**Tone:**
- Professional but conversational
- Active voice (80%+ target)
- Confident and direct

**Formatting:**
- Keep paragraphs to 3-5 sentences
- Use headers for easy scanning
- Bold key terms for emphasis
- Target Flesch score: 50-60

**Length:**
- Aim for 300-500 words (~1 page)
- If exceeding 1 page, consider making a brief or report instead

**Common Pitfalls to Avoid:**
- ❌ Starting with background instead of main point
- ❌ Passive voice ("It was decided" → "We decided")
- ❌ Jargon or acronyms without definition
- ❌ Missing action items or deadlines
- ❌ Paragraphs longer than 5 sentences
