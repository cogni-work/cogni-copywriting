# Professional Proposals

## Quick Reference
**Purpose:** Persuasive business proposals
**Length:** Variable (3-200 pages)
**Formality:** High
**Audience:** Prospects, clients, decision-makers
**Best frameworks:** FAB, PSB, Pyramid
**Key requirement:** Clear value proposition and ROI

## Standard Structure

### With FAB (Consulting Proposal)
```markdown
# [Proposal Title]

## Executive Summary
[Problem → Solution → Value]

## Understanding Your Challenge
[Client's pain points]

## Our Solution

### Feature 1: [Name]
**Feature:** [Technical capability]
**Advantage:** [Why it's superior]
**Benefit:** [Business value with metrics]

### Feature 2: [Name]
[Repeat FAB structure]

## Investment & Timeline
[Pricing and schedule]

## Why Choose Us
[Credentials, case studies]

## Next Steps
[Clear path forward]
```

## Key Characteristics

- **Client-focused:** "You" language, not "We" focus
- **Value-driven:** ROI and benefits quantified
- **Evidence-based:** Case studies, testimonials, data
- **Professional:** High production quality

## Quality Standards

- Executive summary: 1-2 pages with Pyramid/PSB structure
- Benefits quantified with metrics
- FAB structure for 3-5 key features
- Professional formatting with visual elements
